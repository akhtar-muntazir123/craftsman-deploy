Vstore Roto - Vertical Carousels

VSTORE ROTO is a system of rotating shelves that move up or down along a tracking guide in response to operator commands. Items are delivered quickly and directly to the operator, for enhanced throughput and productivity. Our vertical storage and retrieval Systems with a small footprint, high density, V Store Roto makes maximum use of floor space and Offers cost-effective, efficient, quick and ergonomic solutions to meet your storage and retrieval needs.
