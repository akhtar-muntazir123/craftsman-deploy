Slotted Angle Shelving

Craftsman’s Slotted Angle Shelving is designed for faster and easier access to manual storage solutions of light loads. Slotted Shelving systems are easy to assemble and disassemble.

Boltless Shelving

Boltless shelving systems are designed to improve the storage operational time in the warehouse with the elimination of time in the mantling and dismantling of shelves. Boltless shelving accommodates the storage needs of small to multilevel archives.

Long Span Shelving

Long span shelving is the most popular shelving system and is designed to fulfil the customized manual picking and stacking of a variety of materials, bins, etc. with various load and height adjustments with increased space optimization.

Multi Tier Shelving

Craftsman’s Multi-Tier Shelving system enables our customers to maximize storage space optimization and increase the storing and picking of non-palletized goods and accessories stacked in bins or containers. Multi-tier shelving saves the customer's premium floor space by optimizing the available space with shelving systems.

Carton / Bin Live Storage

Craftsman’s Carton Live Storage systems enable compact and well-organized storage solutions applicable for stacking and picking the cartons/bins of various warehousing requirements. It is the most preferred choice in picking the small quantities of unique large volumes at a faster operational time. Carton Live Storage uses gravity for easy stacking.

Mobile Shelving

Craftsman’s Mobile shelving systems deploy versatile and compact storage solutions to fulfil the stacking and storing requirements in most industries. Mobile shelving enables more space savers and easy configuration in racking the large scale file storage for regular compliance. Mobile Shelving storage eliminates the aisle space and deploys easier and faster access to each shelf.

Goods Lift - Vertical Reciprocating Conveyor

Goods Lift is an integral part of the storage system and greatly reduces the time taken in moving the materials between the multi-tier/level shelving with the completely safe and secured transfer. Vertical Reciprocating Conveyor deploys faster and efficient material dispatches in warehouses, factories, industrial plants, institutions and other establishments.

Pick to Light Systems

Craftsman’s Pick to Light System provides a sortation solution with improved picking accuracy and warehousing productivity at a reduced time and labour costs. Pick to light optimizes the warehouse operational time with paperless picking, placing or sorting and assembling products with faster order replenishments.
